# Quality Guardrails

## Intent

Provide baseline quality expectations for architecture artifacts generated or reviewed with AI support.

## Guardrails

- State scope, assumptions, constraints, and open questions.
- Link recommendations to business capabilities and measurable outcomes.
- Capture material decisions in Architecture Decision Records.
- Include non-functional requirements and operational impact.
- Identify integration, data, security, privacy, compliance, and delivery risks.
- Make review criteria explicit before approval.

## Review Standard

An artifact is ready for review when a qualified architect can evaluate the decision, alternatives, risks, and implementation implications without relying on hidden context.
