# Architecture Principles

## Intent

Define durable architecture principles that guide solution decisions across business capability, application, data, integration, security, and operations concerns.

## Principles

1. Business outcomes drive technology choices.
2. Architecture decisions are explicit, traceable, and reviewed.
3. Systems are designed for change, integration, and operational ownership.
4. Risk, security, and compliance are considered from the start.
5. Reuse shared platform capabilities before creating new ones.

## Usage

Use these principles when creating solution designs, reviewing architecture proposals, or resolving design tradeoffs.
