# Architecture AI Maturity Model

## Level 1: Ad Hoc

AI is used informally by individuals without shared standards or reusable artifacts.

## Level 2: Repeatable

Teams use common prompts, templates, and review practices for selected architecture activities.

## Level 3: Managed

AI-assisted architecture work follows governed workflows, traceable decisions, and quality checks.

## Level 4: Integrated

AI agents are connected to architecture repositories, standards, review boards, and delivery processes.

## Level 5: Optimized

Architecture practices continuously improve through measured outcomes, feedback loops, and portfolio-level learning.
