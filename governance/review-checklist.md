# Architecture Review Checklist

## Scope and Context

- Business goals and scope are clear.
- Assumptions, constraints, and dependencies are documented.
- Stakeholders and ownership are identified.

## Architecture Quality

- Key decisions and alternatives are documented.
- Target architecture alignment is assessed.
- Integration, data, security, and operational impacts are covered.
- Non-functional requirements are measurable.

## Risk and Governance

- Material risks have mitigations or owners.
- Exceptions to standards are explicit.
- Open questions and approval conditions are tracked.
